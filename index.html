How to Play

  • When moved to Tableau, the faced up cards can only be transferred in descending order (King to Ace) and in alternating color.
  • Foundations can only be stacked by matching suits and in ascending order.
  • An empty spot in the Tableau may be filled with a king only.
  • Clicking/tapping a card will auto-stack it if an appropriate spot is available.
  • If the player cannot move any cards within the Tableau, the player can select cards from the top of the Stock pile to form the Talon.
  • When the Stock runs out, the Talon is reshuffled to form a new Stock. When Vegas mode is enabled, the player is able to go through the Stock only once.
  • When playing in Vegas mode, the player will have to “buy” the deck, represented by a -52 score at the beginning of every game.
  • To win the game, the player has to build up the Foundations stacks. When all cards have been transferred in ascending order (Ace to King) to the Foundations, the game is won. If no more moves can be made and the Foundations is incomplete, the game is lost.
